About the artist

My name is Cassidy and my passion is art! I enjoy painting, colored pencils, and even pens. Besides art I enjoy baking and the outdoors. I am an animal lover who has a six year old dog. When I'm not being creative I'm usually outside or at the gym. Health is really important to me. One more thing about me is that I am pagan. I hope that my art can inspire more people to be creative.
